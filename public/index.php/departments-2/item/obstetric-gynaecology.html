Obstetric & Gynaecology

Our Obstetric department offers the full spectrum of general and specialty obstetrical care and is a destination of choice for women seeking these services both locally and nationally. Our team not only offers expert patient services; it also includes leaders in basic science, clinical trials, and population-based research.

Services Offered

  • High-risk obstetrics
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine
  • Fertility and reproductive services
  • Gynecologic Oncology
  • Menopause treatment
  • Minimally invasive surgery
  • Hysteroscopy
  • Endometrial ablation (NovaSure)
  • Urogynocological services
  • Non-incisional sterilization for permanent contraception (Essure)
  • Imaging services including MRI, CT and ultrasound
